Job description

The position of Senior Auditor Switzerland.

Main responsibilities

  • Manage individual audit assignments from the planning, fieldwork and delivering high quality findings and recommendations;
  • Interact professionally with senior management in private banking and support functions;
  • Identify areas for improvement in controls and identify when regulations are not being properly implemented;
  • Apply sound internal audit techniques and be able to document conclusions clearly and accurately.

Skills and experience

  • 5-8 years internal audit experience in either big 4 environment or internal audit function of major private bank in Switzerland;
  • Fluency with FINMA regulations. Other jurisdictions a bonus;
  • An undergraduate degree combined with a Chartered Accountant or Chartered Internal Auditor qualification;
  • Proven delivery in complex internal audit engagements;
  • Strong communicating skills and ability to manage differences of opinion satisfactorily;
  • English language proficiency (other Swiss official languages a bonus);
  • Ability to travel within Switzerland and internationally (max 25%).

Our company

EFG Bank is the Swiss private banking subsidiary of EFG International. Headquartered in Zurich, it has an international network spanning Europe, Asia Pacific and Latin America, and manages assets for private clients all over the world. The bank’s services comprise asset management and investment counselling, investment funds, securities brokerage, estate planning and a full array of banking services. EFG Bank offers a stimulating and dynamic work environment.
